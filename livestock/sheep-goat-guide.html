Introduction to Small Ruminant Farming

Sheep and goat farming offers diverse opportunities for both meat and dairy production. These animals are adaptable to various environments and can be managed effectively with proper care and attention.

  • Choose suitable breeds
  • Prepare housing facilities

Production Goals

  • Meat production
  • Dairy products
  • Wool/fiber (sheep)

Breed Selection

Meat Breeds
Breeds optimized for meat production with good growth rates
Dairy Breeds
Breeds known for high milk production and quality
Dual Purpose
Breeds suitable for both meat and milk production

Housing Requirements

  • Adequate space per animal
  • Good ventilation
  • Protection from elements
  • Proper drainage
  • Easy-to-clean surfaces

Fencing and Pasture

  • Secure perimeter fencing
  • Rotational grazing setup
  • Shelter in pastures
  • Water access points

Feed Requirements
